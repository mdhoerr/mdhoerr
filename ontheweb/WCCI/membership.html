Wellness Council Membership Benefits

A Wellness Council membership can save you time, money, and energy while enabling you to design, implement and manage an on-going health promotion program that will impact your corporate culture. The Wellness Council offers two membership options. 
    Employer Member: Organizations offering worksite health promotion programs to their own employees. 

    Vendor Member: organizations and companies which can provide support services to a worksite wellness program.
