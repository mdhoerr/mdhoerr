WELCOA 
The Wellness Councils of America (WELCOA) is a national nonprofit membership organization dedicated to promoting healthier lifestyles, especially through health promotion activities at the worksite. Members gain access to health products and resources, newsletters, WELL CITY USA,  Well Workplace programs, and more.
