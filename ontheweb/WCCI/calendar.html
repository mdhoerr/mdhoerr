Assessing the Impact of Your Wellness Program
March 2, 2000             1:00 - 4:00 p.m.
Members: $20             Non-members: $49
Location: Encompass Health Management, 4949 Westown Parkway, WDM, IA
The key to long-term success is having the ability to demonstrate your program's impact on your organization. Evaluation starts wherever you are. You can be a program evaluator without being a researcher! This workshop is for all program levels; beginners to advanced. You will identify how to gather useful information, make sense of it and understand how to use it to improve your program.
