ESFJ Home Pages 
Father Terry's Verbal Conscience 
(added 24 Nov 1999)   
has a current picture of Father Terry opening a church door on the left, a well organized table of contents in the middle, and a photo on the right that changes from baby pictures on through various ages. He put off joining the priesthood until he had raised his four nieces, whose parents had died. Most of the site is devoted to various topics and questions related to his parish work. 
Entrance to Abigail's World   
(added 1 Mar 1999)   
First you get her entrance page. Click on the graphic, and you get the table of contents page. The contents include Personal Info, Wedding Photos, Resume, Hobbies, Trips, Net People & Places, Useful web sites, Credits, and Mail Me. Every item includes a verb: not just "Personal Info" but "Read some Personal Info about me". Her Personal Info page is called "All About Abigail: Like Hints from Heloise, but ... different." So well organized I don't think even I could get lost here. Her hobbies page wore me out: she has more book reviews than I do in the Reading section of her hobby page alone! She likes reading, beadwork, cooking, travel, computer games ... A very attractive web site.  
Konstantinov's Home Page   
(added 26 Jul 1999)  
She got the nickname "Konstantinov" when she played hockey in high school. Her home page is long list of links, starting with a link to her picture page. The picture page is a list of her photos, and she apologizes for having only 43 pictures listed. Besides her friends, she's interested in horses, sports, and nutrition. She's got one of the longest lists of band-related links I've seen on a personal page.
