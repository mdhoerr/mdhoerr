Changing Churches, Changing Type 
The Discipling Dilemma, by Flavil R. Yeakley, Jr., Editor, Howard W. Norton, Don E. Vinzant and Gene Vinzant is now available online. Chapter Two of the book provides the results of MBTI studies done on members of the Boston Church of Christ, with that church's permission. The study followed type development among members of that church. It found that members of the Church were reporting a change in type, and that the change converged toward one type: ESFJ.
