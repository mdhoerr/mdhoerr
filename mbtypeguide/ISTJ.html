ISTJ on Finding her Mother   
(updated 16 Feb 2000)  
Added on 7 Jun 1999, this article has moved. The link has been updated to the new location.  
In Finding My Mother: How Hawaii Five-0 has influenced my life, Leilani Kimmel-Dagostino describes how she used the investigative techniques of Steve McGarrett from the old Hawaii Five-0 television series to find her mother. Raised by her father, and told that her mother had died of natural causes, she later found out her mother was still alive, and determined to find her. Her story illustrates two ISTJ investigators at work - her fictional mentor, McGarrett, and Kimmel-Dagostino herself.
