Scott McCulloch's Homepage (a.k.a. Staple's Playground)
(added 17 May 2000)  
A two-color picture of him is at the top of the page. Mouseover to get full color - click to get his interests which range from Golf to Squeezing jelly donuts to Lycanthropic baying. If you stay on his main page instead, past the animated do-hickeys, the rest of the page is mostly one-liners. Most of the links are to funny pages that he couldn't put in one line.
Benny's Home Page 
(added 8 Dec 1999)  
The first screen of his home page contains his photo, a table of contents, and a short welcome with almost every word clickable. There are several animations - click on the enter animation, and you come to another page with a table of contents, and a more chatty welcome - again with lots of clickable links in the narrative. His interests are scuba diving, travel, waterskiing, ice hockey, photography, jet skiing, tennis, golf and in line skating. 
Keith Rogers' Unwilling Home Page  
(added 17 May 1999)  
He only has this homepage up because he is working on computerizing a personality test. You can help him by participating in his questionaire, or you can go to his harp-related links. I thought it interesting that he was pretty evenly split on the N vs S function, leaving him to choose between INTP and ISTP. He decided the SP temperament described him better, so he's an ISTP. I did the same thing on INFP vs INTP. The NT temperament fit me much better than the NF.
