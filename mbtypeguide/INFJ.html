Private INFJ List (subscription upon request) (added 15 Mar 2000) 
The list owner describes the list this way: There's no better way to learn more about the INFJ personality than to join the INFJ-list! This is a very lively mailing list with a total of more than a hundred members; list traffic varies from moderate to high, with discussions ranging from the philosophical and religious to bunnies, chocolates, Star Trek, Jung, MBTI Type and Temperament theory, to relationships - plus everything inbetween! It's difficult to accurately convey the feel of a mailing list, so if you're interested, by all means join now and see for yourself. There is no such thing as "off-topic" on the friendly INFJ-list. Conflict and cynical arguments or flames are not welcome, however.
