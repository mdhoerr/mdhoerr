It's The CHEESE !!!  
(added 3 May 2000) 
Underneath a graphic of " IT'S THE CHEESE" spelled out in cheese (of course), the site starts with "IT'S NICE TO MEET YOU !!! Thanks for stopping by. My name is Gary but my friends call me Cheese." Under that is his picture, a story of how he got his nickname along with some cheese wordplays, and then a section called "Who is GaryCheese?" His first paragraph gives his educational and professional history, then he starts over by giving his astrological sign and his MB type. After a few lines about summer camp we finally find out he's the drummer in a band, which of course leads to what music he listens to, concerts he goes to, road trips and travel. Finally, at the bottom of the page, you find out what he does for a living: marketing. Below that more pictures. His statement that "I believe is that luck is something that you create yourself" and list of four favorite books is about as philosophical as the site gets. Lots of concrete info, lots of links out embedded in the conversational style.

A Lincoln's Doctor's Dog Homepage  
(added 9 Aug 1999)   
is the name of Sandra Weinhardt's personal webspace. She calls it that because someone once advised a writer to include Lincoln, dogs and physicians in a book to make it a bestseller, and she wants her webspace to be a free bestseller. This web page is just a jumping off place to her webspaces devoted to different topics, from her five cyberzines to several other interest areas. You could spend days wandering through her various topics and areas, ranging from James Dean, rockabilly and mysteries to serious topics like bereavement and violence against women. Her type info is on her one of her links pages.
