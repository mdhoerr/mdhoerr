INTP Home Pages  
adniL.com
(added 22 Mar 2000)
This site was recommended as an INTP resource. The top of the page says "Welcome to the fabulous, megalomaniacal adniL.com", followed by a picture of her. Then there is a list of her interests, in no particular order, ranging from quilts to computer projects to eye surgery. In true INTP fashion, you will discover where she went to school, that she had eye surgery, and get a list of books, with ratings, that she's read before you'll find her name, which you won't even find it on her "What is this adniL person like?" page.
Paul James Home Page   
(added 5 Jul 1999)   
In true INTP fashion (based on my own example - a sample of one), his home page is only one of several web sites, and probably not the most important.. He is a computer freak, a Jesus freak, one quarter Welsh, loves music, weather, is a photography phreak and a sci-fi geek. Other webpages he has authored are listed at Paul James' Web Pages, and include pages devoted to several styles of music, weather, photography, faith and the MBTI® instrument. I got the Myers-Briggs™ jokes above at one of his sites. 
Karl Foelsche's (The Internet Surfur's) Home Page   
(added 8 Mar 1999)   
The first section on his home page is "A Word or Two About Me". The first paragraph tells you exactly what Karl's career goals are, how he is qualified, what his recent experience is, and points you to his resume. Next comes a paragraph on his wife and marriage, then a paragraph on type, then one pointing to his humor page. The next section is on Computers, with a list of links to computer sites he reads regularly. Then come sections on Games and Music, each section pointing to a page with information and links. The next section is Travel, which points to page with his travel log and photos from a trip to Peru. The last section is on Academia, and points you to a page with a paper he wrote on TQM and VIP. Some things you won't find out: where he was born, his favorite color, what kind of car he drives, or his favorite sports team.
