Past Link Reviews

Company has Info in French, German, Nederlands
Alert Management Consultants is active in several fields of personnel management. The common goal of their staff is to coach and stimulate individuals in organizations. They have offices in Belgium, Germany and the Netherlands, and provide MBTI® instruments in French, German, and Nederlands.

MBTI® Instrument in Danish (added 27 Septemer 2000)
Dansk Management Forum provides both MBTI® materials and authorisation courses in Danish. You can visit their English page, or look over their MBTI® offerings in Danish. They describe themselves as "an independent networking organisation striving to inspire and support business and management development across sectors, branches and businesses."

MBTI® Instrument in Swedish (added 20 Sep 2000)
Psykologiförlaget AB provides MBTI® materials in Swedish. Psykologiförlaget is the Swedish member of the European Test Publishers Group. You can choose to read some information in English, but most of it is in Swedish.

MBTI® Info in Czech (added 13 Sep 2000)
I got an email asking me to find Myers-Briggs™ info in Czech, so I starting looking. This article, Hadej, hadej kdo je to? by Marek Housa, describes the Myers-Briggs™ functions in Czech.

MBTI® Products in Norwegian (added 13 Sep 2000)
Optimas Organisasjonspsykologene AS provides MBTI® materials in Norwegian. This includes the first (I think) type book in Norwegian, Typeforståelse, and MBTI® version G instrument in Norwegian. They also provide qualifying workshops. Look around the site, and you'll also find a short introduction to type in Norwegian as well.

MBTI® Intrument Publisher in France (added 6 Sep 2000)
The MBTI® instrument is distributed in France by Les Editions du Centre de Psychologie Appliquée (ECPA), which is the main tests publisher in France. The ECPA has been bought by the Weka Company Group, one of the major European publishing groups. I was unable to find a web page about the ECPA in French, but you can find a page of information in English at the European Test Publishers Group (scroll down to the bottom of the page to find the ECPA link). Information about ECPA from the Weka Company Group is available in German at here and in English here.

Get Qualified to Administer the MBTI® Instrument in French (added 6 Sep 2000)
Osiris Conseil, whose site is available in both English and French, is the company in France which does all the development and qualifying training on the MBTI® instrument in French. Company founders Geneviève Cailloux and Pierre Cauvin have translated the MBTI® intrument and the MBTI® Manual by Isabel Myers and Mary McCaulley into French, as well as several well-known type books, including books by Susan Brock, Sandra Hirsch and Jean Kummerow, among others. They have also written several books of their own about the MBTI® instrument. Osiris Conseil has trained and qualified 1300 people to use the MBTI® instrument.

Myers-Briggs™ Material in Norway (added 19 Jul 2000)
Kontura is a Norwegian company that provides MBTI® services. It appears they also provide qualification courses for the MBTI® instrument, although I'm not sure since I don't read Norwegian.

Sports Training Programs by Type (added 14 Jun 2000)
Apparently based on Niednagel's work on the relationship between sports performance and type, Sport Consulting Team (SCT) provides coaching programs based on "Action Type ®." At least I think so - the site is in Dutch, and I could read some of it because it is close to German. There is the usual sixteen cell type table, which takes you to information for each type.

Executive Digest - Digital, in Portuguese (added 24 May 2000)
Aol.com is an article about Bill Gates, Steve Case, and Paul Allen, but a sidebar discusses type. I think it was saying that one or more of them were INTP (Bill Gates is usually typed as INTP).

Voice of America Page (added 12 Apr 2000)
No, I can't read it, but I can tell that it is about Myers-Briggs™. It's a Voice of America Croatian page called Mayers-Briggsov pokazatelj.

Povolzhsky Interregional Educational Center (added 9 Feb 2000)
Here's an intro to type in Russian, if you have the right fonts installed. In her article, Inquiry of Myers Briggs into the Practice of Professional Counselling, published in 1998 Volume 2 of the Human Resources Journal put out by the Povolzhsky Interregional Educational Center, T. Shalaeva provides a short introduction to Myers-Briggs™ type, and then discusses how it may apply to job counselling situations.

Statistics on MB type in France (en français) (added 27 Oct 1999) 
Sybac provides training in intercultural aspects of management for large companies and helps small businesses to consider cultural specifics in their approach to sales and marketing in foreign countries. They provide quite a bit of MBTI® info in French. According to their site, the organization that certifies MBTI® practitioners in France is the Centre de Psychologie Appliquée à Paris (.I was unable to find a url for them).  Sybac quotes French MBTI® statistics that the NF temperament is the most common (38.2%) temperament in France. 

Myers Briggs info en español (added 13 Oct 1999) 
Francisco Morfín Otero is Jefe del departamento de Electrónica, Sistemas e Informática. His page with Myers-Briggs™ information is called 7.1 EL EQUIPO DEL PROYECTO Y EL GRUPO HUMANO.  (may no longer be available as of 29 May 2000)

Dipartimento di Psicologia dell'Università di Bologna (added 27 Oct 1999) 
The Dipartimento di Psicologia dell'Università di Bologna has MBTI® info in Italian on their website. Since I can't read Italian, I was unable to determine if they referenced an organization that administers the MBTI® instrument in Italian, or whether they can administer it themselves. 

More Myers-Briggs™ en Español (added 20 Oct 1999, updated 24 May 2000) 
Update: the Spanish Myers-Briggs™ info page is no longer available.
Ernesto Baca, INTP, is an independent groundwater consultant specializing in computer modeling and hydrologic data interpretation. He also has a little bit of Myers-Briggs™ info in Spanish (scroll down the page).

Personal Potential (added 8 Sep 1999) 
provides career management services using personality type and several other factors. The site is available in Spanish and Dutch as well as English. They also have some interesting correlations between type and entrepreneurship, charisma, being a team player, and other career-related characteristics.  
"On Personal Potential's website, you can determine on-line your success-factors and consequently get an impression of your potential and self-image. Having received your results, you can find the answers to important FAQ's concerning your personality. The report PPA Personal - acting as a sophisticated career management tool - provides you with a sound career advice, guarantees an effective approach of the labour market, whilst giving you both the tools to create a highly distinctive CV and the ingredients to write a convincing application letter." 

The Keirsey Temperament Sorter II (updated 9 Aug 1999) 
Temperament Sorter copyrighted by David Keirsey in 1998. 70 either-or items, scored immediately at the end of the test. Determine whether you are a Guardian ["SJ"], Artisan ["SP"], Idealist ["NF"], or Rational ["NT"]. Keirsey also has descriptive terms for each four-letter type. For example, I'm a Designer. The sorter is also available online at this site in German, Spanish, Portuguese and Norwegian. 

Menk Privates Bildungsinstitut (added 9 Aug 1999) 
is a German company where you can get the indicator and information about Myers-Briggs™ in German. Their intro is "Do you know what you want?" They say that: (my translation)  
We accompany people in Teams and Organizations, that wish to develop themselves, in that we support mental change processes, so that new business in systematic complexity is made possible. As "learning organizations", they can successfully shape the demands of the future, in that people experience that they create their own reality and thus also can change it.  
original:  
Wir begleiten Menschen in Teams und Organisationen, die sich entwickeln wollen, indem wir mentale Veränderungsprozesse unterstützen, damit neues Handeln in systemischer Komplexität ermöglicht wird. Als "lernende Organisationen" können sie die Herausforderungen der Zukunft erfolgreich gestalten, da die Menschen erleben, daß sie ihre Realität selbst erschaffen und somit auch verändern können.  

Psychometrics (added 12 Jul 1999) 
is one of Canada's leading publishers of tests and assessment services for business, education and government. They provide the MBTI® testing materials to those qualified to administer them. They have a French section: section francaise. 

Bienvenue sur les pages de DJëdï. (added 12 Jul 1999)  
His site is in French, and like the other INTJ home pages, is mainly a list of organized links. One of his interests is type, and he has a French type test on his site. 

Conseiller en Orientation (added 12 Jul 1999) 
Jules Viviers calls himself "l'orienteur", and he is a "Conseiller en Orientation". His site is in French, and he has a couple of pages with MBTI® information and philosophy in French. 

Insights Training & Development Ltd  (added 5 Jul 1999) 
is an International Training organisation with its head office in Dundee, Scotland. Their focus appears to be more on the eight Jungian types than on the Myers-Briggs™ types. They have an active site with a forum, newsletter, and free sample reports. I particularly liked the sample personal report at the Resources tab (available English, Finnish, French, German, Portuguese, Spanish), which appeared to be based on an ISFJ type. 

CC&S Management Consultants (added 15 Mar 2000)
"is dedicated to support teams and their coaches by guiding and facilitating them to become a successful organization where people want to work." One aspect of their services includes using the MBTI® instrument. They are headquartered in the Netherlands, and their site is available in English and Dutch.
