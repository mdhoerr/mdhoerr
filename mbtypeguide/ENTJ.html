ENTJFemales Egroup (added 6 Sep 2000)   
Thanks to the subscriber to the Myers-Briggs™ Personality Type on the Web newsletter who sent me ENTJFemales as a recommended link for ENTJ's. Founded in April 1999, this egroup has 18 members and regular message activity (4 or 5 messages a month are typical, but there have been some months with over 50 messages.) According to the description of the group: "This is an email group where you can discuss the joys, trials and tribulations of being an ENTJ female. Sometimes you may feel like giving in to your natural feminity. At other times, you may feel more like the traditional male by taking charge. How do you balance these two roles? How do you make friends with other females when your personality characteristics are more male than female? With this email group, you will find that you are not alone. You will be able to exchange ideas, stories and support. You will feel right at home with other ENTJ females."

Solace   
(added 25 Aug 1999)  
is Eric Cheng's web space. Name, birthdate, photo and four entry points: Bio, Resume, Interests and Shareware. Best use of a personal photo I've seen: his picture blends into the background and seems to "speak" the works (no, I'm not talking balloons or audio here). Click on Bio to get the personal info, including a simple listing of his MB type. The top looks like a picture ID format with a twist: if you can use javascript, you can actually choose a photo of Eric from one of several past years. Next is a link to what seems to be an online journal. Then welcome to his life - computers, family and spare time interests.
